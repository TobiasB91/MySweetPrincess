How to run the prototype:
Open the project in Unity, set Game View to full screen, press Play Button.

How to play:
Move with the arrow keys.
Move the camera with w,a,s,d.
Change camera view with q and e.

Your goal is to collect all the candy without starving or getting too fat to move
You starve when your weight is 30.
You are too fat to move when your weight is 100.
Moving costs 1 weight point when moving on the same plane or dropping one plane.
Moving up one plane costs 2 weight points, you can't climb or drop two or more planes.
Moving in the deep water costs 5 weight points.

You sink in the water when your Avatar gets fat (at wieght = 75)
When your weight is 50 or below the fan can blow you away.
